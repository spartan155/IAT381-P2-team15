VESSEL OVERVIEW

The marathon heavy-cruiser is a legacy ship from the Eridani system. In operation 10,000 years ago it relied on bulk heading rather than shielding to absorb damage. This refitted classic model keeps the same bulkheads which gave the Marathon its reputation as a punishment absorber as well as outfitting it with modern shielding and proton-torpedoes.
