VESSEL OVERVIEW

10 turbo-laser batteries and 2 ion cannons allow the corvette to punch above its tonnage taking on ships many times its size. Although Sienar has designed the corvette with a combat role in mind it has become popular amongst senators as diplomatic transport ships as they may be fitted with a variety of furnishings and also protects them wherever they go. The Corvette's engines also have a very high sub-light speed as well as above average Super-luminal velocities making it popular with traders and as a light combat vessel.
