VESSEL OVERVIEW

The Defender class cruiser served as a Republic stike-cruiser during the Cold War with the Sith Empire. Their position as command ships took full advantage of their advanced shielding and comm-relays to coordinate and lead attacks on armored convoys. Its laser cannons can be overloaded to produce a piercing shot albiet at the chance of melted circuitry. The Defender is also useful as a long range recon vessel and was often used on secret missions by the Republic on long range assignment with skeleton crews.
