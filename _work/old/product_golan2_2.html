VESSEL OVERVIEW

The Golan-II battle station is a staple of the Imperial planetary garrison. Operating primarily as planetary defence weapons platforms these stations are capable of combatting an Imperial star destroyer. They have hangar space for 4 squadrons of TIE/ln fighters and the capability to have 4 star destroyers docked simultaneously. The Empire often uses the Golan-II station to guard their most high risk naval bases such as Bilbrigi Shipyards as well as Kuat and Fondor.
