About Sienar Fleet Systems

Bringing you only the finest in starfleet systems and support for over fifteen millennia.

Sienar fleet systems are the primary supplier and research group of the Galactic Empire’s support and logistics division. While we supply the Imperial navy with the most cutting edge advances in aerospace and propulsion we also develop and manufacture commercial starship systems for mercantile ventures and personal use.

Sienar fleet systems has always been there to answer the call to arms with the efficiency and quality granted by being the galaxy’s technology leader. As the premier designer of starship platforms and propulsion technology, Sienar has gained a household name as one of the greatest innovators in starship design and production in the galaxy.

The cornerstone of our beliefs is the modular design of our star fighter air-frames which has become the benchmark against which bulk fighters are measured. Proceeding from the classic Tie X-1 chassis we have diversified the Twin Ion Engine star fighter into a wide variety of combat and non-combat roles ranging from reconnaissance to interceptor-fighters to strategic bombing roles. The TIE family has become one of the most recognizable starships in space and its performance reputation makes it a terrifying sight for anyone who engages it.

The ion engine plays an important role in many of Sienar’s sub-light ship designs and we are dedicated to improving ion performance focusing on maximum interchangeability of parts and ease of maintenance. As a result, Sienar propulsion systems are ideal for the battlefield and can be deployed in any number of combat roles; remaining in peak performance with minimal battle maintenance and perfect for rapid troop deployment.
