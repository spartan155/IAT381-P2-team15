VESSEL OVERVIEW

The Imperial-II class star destroyer has become the backbone of the Imperial navy. This star destroyer is a potent war-machine the match of any other battleship in the galaxy. The Imperial navy has congratulated Sienar on the quality of the star destroyer as it has been documented surviving 4 on 1 battles while still being able to navigate to a dry-dock for repairs under its own power.

Powered by a solar ion reactor, the star destroyer maneuvers with 8 military-thrust Ion engines which produce over 100,000 megajoules of kinetic energy. It carries a compliment of 72 Tie starfighters with room for a variety of additional vessels. The maximum length of ship which can be accomidated by its hangar is 150 meters meaning the star destroyer could carry an entire gunship with it.
