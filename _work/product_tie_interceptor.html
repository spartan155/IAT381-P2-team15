VESSEL OVERVIEW

Extremely agile and, as with the Tie/ln Fighter, completely unshielded. The lack of shielding is made up for by the Tie Interceptor's small silhouette and blinding sublight speeds. It sports four laser cannons on the tips of its wings and is so maneuverable that a skilled pilot can literally fly circles around a T-65 X-wing.

The four solar panels collect ionizing radiation from any solar source converting it into the very fuel and electrical energy which powers its twin ion engines. This effectively grants the Interceptor unlimited fuel for combat engagement at the cost of removing the hyperdrive generator. This tradeoff is necessary as we have reduced weight by eliminating the need for a generator and reducing the overall size and complexity of the ship design to serve its role more fully. The lack of exotic parts makes repair and maintanence much cheaper, less time consuming and requiring less technical expertise in a combat situation.
