Product Description:The Lambda class shuttle is a transport ship favoured by the Imperial Navy. Its distinct shape identifies it as a diplomatic ship capable of advanced space and atmospheric maneuvering. This is due to Sienar’s efforts to reduce computer lag-time switching between main thrusters and repulsor lifts during flight. While normally this is a pilot controlled feature the Lambda has automated the process allowing shuttle pilots to perform maneuvers without worrying about flight modes. The shuttle sports two high energy level ball laser turrets and shielding to protect pilot and passengers from attack. Its interior contains many amenities allowing dignitaries to travel in style.
