Product Description:The TX-1138 pod racer was a concept air-frame designed by Sienar to race in the Boonta-eve classic. Utilizing a trans-plast base we were able to greatly reduce the weight and strain on the repulsor panels and transplant a modular Sienar Ion engine as the source of thrust. The pod produced a special challege for Sienar in designing an aerodynamic hull. As our specialty is space we were interested in this project for its unique technical challenges.
